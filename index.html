When one is ready to metamorphose, it stops feeding and makes its way to the surface, generally at night. It adapts to reality with its head out of the water, while its respiration system evolves to utilize digital layers. Morph-Cut is a path to change and project your identity. Characters no longer remain permanent in our stories. Their physical and emotional layers are constantly reshaped together with the environment. Morph-Cut is a powerful tool to change the perception of us, giving previously unrelated and random events profound connections and helping build the fundamental structures of both: the world we live in and the virtual. Be whoever and wherever you want through our unstable fantasy sequence.
